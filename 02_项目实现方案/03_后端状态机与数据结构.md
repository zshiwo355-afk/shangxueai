## 四、状态机

阶段：
- opening
- need_probe
- brand_trust
- product_intro
- price_discuss
- objection
- closing
- after_sale
- finished

状态变化主要由 LLM 每轮评分输出 `suggested_next_stage`，后端校验后更新。

## 五、成交硬判断

后端可使用：

```python
def can_deal(session):
    e = session["emotion_state"]
    a = session["completed_actions"]
    return (
        session["round_count"] >= 10
        and e["trust"] >= 70
        and e["interest"] >= 65
        and e["deal_willingness"] >= 65
        and e["price_resistance"] <= 55
        and a["recommended_product"]
        and a["handled_objection"]
        and a["attempted_close"]
    )
```

此函数不代替知识库评分，只作为最终安全校验。
